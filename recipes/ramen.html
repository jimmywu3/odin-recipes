Tomato Egg Ramen

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Green onion
  • Eggs
  • Ramen
  • Olive oil, oregano, salt, pepper
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fish sauce, soy sauce
  • Optional: Shrimp

Steps

  1. Cut Tomato, Green Onion, 3 Cloves of Garlic
  2. Crack 2 Eggs and mix with 1/4 tsp salt
  3. In pot add 1 tbsp oil and 1 tbsp minced garlic
  4. Cook on low heat
  5. Add white part of green onion
  6. Once you smell garlic and green onion add tomato
  7. Add 1 tsp oregano, lil salt n' pepper, 2 tbsp tomato sauce, 1/4 tbsp red pepper powder, 1 tbsp of fish sauce, and 1/2 tbsp of soy sauce
  8. Mix ingredients
  9. Add 550 ml water
  10. Change to medium heat and boil the water
  11. Taste and add salt accordingly
  12. Add ramen when soup boiling
  13. When noodles almost cooked add egg mixture in a circle
  14. Optional: Add shrimp
  15. Add green onion
  16. Serve!
